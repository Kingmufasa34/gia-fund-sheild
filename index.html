Capital at Risk: What You Must Know

Radical transparency. Here's exactly what can go wrong.

You Can Lose Your Entire Investment

If a trigger event occurs early (Month 1-2), you may lose 95-100% of capital. While probability is low (2% annually based on historical data), loss severity is high. This is high-risk investing.

No FSCS Protection

Unlike cash ISAs, these investments are NOT protected by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme. If things go wrong, you cannot claim government compensation up to £85K.

Historical Probabilities Are Not Guarantees

Our 2% trigger probability is based on 20 years of weather/CAT data. Climate change may alter these patterns unpredictably. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

Capital Locked for 12 Months

Once invested, your principal is locked until maturity. While you receive monthly coupons, you cannot withdraw principal early except through limited secondary market (if available).

Not Suitable for Everyone

This product is NOT suitable for:

  • Emergency funds or essential savings
  • Anyone who cannot afford to lose their entire investment
  • Investors with less than £50K liquid net worth
  • Primary retirement savings (should be 5-10% of portfolio maximum)
  • Anyone seeking guaranteed returns or capital preservation

Suitability Check Required

Before investing, you must complete a suitability questionnaire. We will limit investment amounts based on your stated net worth and risk tolerance. This is for your protection and regulatory compliance.

This is a conceptual presentation and not an offer to invest. Any actual investment would be subject to full FCA-compliant disclosure documents, suitability assessments, risk warnings, and cooling-off periods. Regulatory approval required before launch.
