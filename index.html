Capital at Risk: What You Must Know

We believe in radical transparency. Here's exactly what can go wrong.

Warning: You Can Lose Your Entire Investment

If a parametric trigger event occurs early in the term (Month 1-2), you may lose 95% to 100% of your capital. While probability is low (2% annually based on historical data), the loss severity is high.

Warning: No FSCS Protection

Unlike cash ISAs, these investments are NOT protected by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme. If things go wrong, you cannot claim government compensation.

Warning: Historical Probabilities Are Not Guarantees

Our 2% trigger probability is based on 20 years of weather/CAT data. Climate change may alter these patterns. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

Warning: Capital Locked for 12 Months

Once invested, your principal is locked until maturity. While you receive monthly coupons, you cannot withdraw the principal early except through limited secondary market (if available).

Warning: Not Suitable for Everyone

This product is NOT suitable for:

  • Emergency funds or essential savings
  • Anyone who cannot afford to lose their entire investment
